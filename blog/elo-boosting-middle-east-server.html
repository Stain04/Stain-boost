What Makes the ME Server Unique

The Middle East server — officially launched by Riot to serve players across Egypt, Saudi Arabia, UAE, Jordan, Kuwait, Bahrain, Oman, Iraq, and surrounding countries — has a distinct ranked environment that sets it apart from EUW or NA.

Highly Aggressive Playstyle

ME server lobbies are known for their fast, skirmish-heavy gameplay. Players prioritise early kills and lane dominance over scaling, which makes the average game shorter and more chaotic than on Western servers. For a booster, this means champion selection is critical — champions that can snowball early and close out games efficiently perform significantly better here than late-game scalers.

Smaller Active Player Pool

Compared to EUW or EUNE, the ME server has a smaller ranked player base. In higher elo, this means you frequently face the same players — everyone in Diamond and above knows each other. In lower elo, it means matchmaking occasionally widens its MMR window during off-peak hours, which can produce uneven games. A booster who plays on ME server daily understands these patterns and times sessions accordingly.

Regional Latency Expectations

A booster connecting from outside the region will have noticeably higher ping, which directly impacts mechanical performance on fast champions. A local booster connecting from within the Middle East plays at native latency — typically 15–40ms — which means full mechanical output on every champion. This is not a minor detail: at 120ms ping, certain combos and reaction-based plays become physically impossible.

How to Choose the Right Boost Service for ME Server

The ME server boost market has grown over the past few years, and not every service is equal. Here's what to look for — and what to avoid:

Green Flags

  • Booster is verifiably ranked on the ME server (check their profile on OP.GG)
  • Can show recent games played on ME server — not just claims
  • Specific VPN protocols for ME region, not generic "VPN used"
  • Real reviews from ME server players (Discord screenshots, Trustpilot, etc.)
  • Direct communication with the booster — not a ticketing system

Red Flags

  • Prices that seem impossibly cheap — usually signals bot-based boosting
  • No verifiable rank on ME server specifically
  • Automated platform with no direct booster contact
  • No clear explanation of how they handle VPN and location matching
  • Reviews that are clearly template-written or suspiciously uniform

The ME server test: Before ordering from any service, search the booster's claimed IGN on OP.GG for the Middle East region. If their profile doesn't show active games at the rank they claim, walk away. Anyone can claim to be Diamond — it takes two seconds to verify.
